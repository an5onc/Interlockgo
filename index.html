Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ignition interlock devices in Colorado.

Do you offer same-day ignition interlock installation?

Yes. We frequently have same-day or next-day appointments available in Evans, CO. Call 970-515-5740 to check today's openings.

Are you certified for Lifesafer and Guardian devices?

Yes. InterlockGo NOCO is an authorized provider for Lifesafer and Guardian installations, calibrations, service, and clean removals.

What areas do you serve in Northern Colorado?

We serve Evans, Greeley, Fort Collins, Loveland, Windsor, Johnstown, and all of Weld County. Our shop is conveniently located at 3610 35th Avenue Unit #7 in Evans, CO.

How long does an interlock installation take?

A typical installation takes between 2–4 hours. You're welcome to wait on-site or drop off your vehicle and we'll call you when it's ready.

How much does an ignition interlock cost in Colorado?

Costs vary by device and program length. Installation fees and monthly lease/calibration fees apply. Call us at 970-515-5740 for a personalized quote — we'll walk you through all the costs upfront with no surprises.

Do you offer notary services for the interlock affidavit?

Yes! We have a notary on-site so you can get your Colorado interlock affidavit notarized during your appointment — no extra trips needed.

What do I need to bring to my installation appointment?

Bring your vehicle, a Colorado-issued photo ID, and proof of Colorado Registration. We'll handle the rest and help you understand the next steps.

How often do I need to get my interlock device calibrated?

In Colorado, calibration is typically required every 45 days. We'll set up your recurring appointments so you never miss a deadline.
