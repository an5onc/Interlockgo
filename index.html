How It Works

For your driving privileges to be reinstated, you must complete the Colorado state interlock program.

1

Schedule

Call or Visit our shop to schedule an installation appointment.

2

Install

The installation can take our technicians between 2-4 hours. You are welcome to wait on-site or drop off your vehicle.

3

Drive

Present your notarized interlock affidavit and other needed documents to the DMV. Pass the written and driving test to be issued a restricted Driver's License. Get back to your daily routine with confidence.
