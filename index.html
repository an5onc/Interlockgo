How much does an ignition interlock device cost in Colorado?

Costs vary by provider and device. Typically you'll pay an installation fee plus monthly service fees for calibration. Contact us at 970-515-5740 for current pricing on Lifesafer and Guardian devices.

How long do I need an ignition interlock in Colorado?

The interlock period in Colorado depends on your offense. First-time DUI offenders typically need the device for 8 months to 2 years. Repeat offenses require longer periods. Your reinstatement letter from Colorado DMV will specify your exact requirement.
