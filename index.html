Ignition Interlock FAQs

Do you offer same‑day ignition interlock installation?

Yes. We often have same‑day or next‑day appointments available. Call 970‑515‑5740 to check today's openings.

Are you certified for Lifesafer and Guardian devices?

Yes, we are an authorized provider for both Lifesafer and Guardian devices—installation, calibration, and service.

How much does an ignition interlock device cost in Colorado?

Costs vary by provider and device. Typically you'll pay an installation fee plus monthly service fees for calibration. Contact us at 970-515-5740 for current pricing on Lifesafer and Guardian devices.

How long do I need an ignition interlock in Colorado?

The interlock period in Colorado depends on your offense. First-time DUI offenders typically need the device for 8 months to 2 years. Repeat offenses require longer periods. Your reinstatement letter from Colorado DMV will specify your exact requirement.

What areas do you serve in Northern Colorado?

We serve Evans, Greeley, Fort Collins, Loveland, Windsor, Johnstown, and all of Weld County. Our shop is conveniently located at 3610 35th Avenue in Evans, CO.

Can you notarize my interlock affidavit?

Yes! We have a Notary Public on-site. We can notarize your Colorado ignition interlock affidavit during your installation or calibration appointment—no extra trip needed.
