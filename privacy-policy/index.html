Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

InterlockGo NOCO ("we," "us," or "our"), a DBA of Kawika Capitol Ltd LLC, operates the website interlockgo.io and provides ignition interlock installation, calibration, and service in Evans, CO. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you book an appointment, contact us, or use our services:

  • Name — to identify you and your account
  • Phone number — to send appointment reminders, confirmations, and service-related communications via SMS/text message
  • Email address — to send appointment confirmations and service updates
  • Vehicle information — make, model, and year, as required for interlock device installation
  • Appointment details — date, time, service type, and provider information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely for the following purposes:

  • Appointment reminders — sending SMS/text message reminders about upcoming appointments
  • Service communications — confirmations, rescheduling notices, calibration reminders, and follow-ups directly related to your interlock service
  • Customer support — responding to your questions and service requests
  • Review requests — sending a one-time request for feedback after your appointment (you may opt out at any time)

3. SMS/Text Messaging

By providing your phone number and opting in to text messages, you consent to receive service-related SMS messages from InterlockGo NOCO. These messages may include:

  • Appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Calibration due date reminders
  • Review requests after service

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance or contact us at 970-515-5740.

We do not use your phone number for marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — with LifeSafer and Guardian interlock providers as required to fulfill your interlock service obligations
  • SMS delivery — with our text messaging service provider (Twilio) solely to deliver messages to you
  • Legal requirements — if required by law, court order, or government regulation

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill legal obligations:

  • Active clients: Information is retained for the duration of your interlock service program
  • After service completion: Records are retained for up to 3 years for regulatory compliance and customer support, then securely deleted
  • Opt-out requests: If you opt out of SMS, your phone number is removed from our messaging list immediately. You may request full deletion of your data at any time.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. All data is stored in secure, access-controlled systems.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
