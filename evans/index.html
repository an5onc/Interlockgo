Do I need an appointment?

Which interlock brands do you service?

We're certified installers for both Lifesafer and Guardian ignition interlock devices. We can help you choose the right device based on your requirements and budget.

How often do I need to come in?

Colorado requires calibration every 30-60 days depending on your specific requirements. We'll set up a schedule that keeps you compliant with your court order.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Bring your driver's license, court paperwork, vehicle registration, and payment method. If switching providers, bring your current device paperwork.
